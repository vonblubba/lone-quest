**Lone Quest** is a personal blog where I journal my solo TTRPG adventures.

I use several different systems, but I prefer SciFi and Horror settings (*Call of Cthulhu, Delta Green, Imperium Maledictum, Mothership* to name a few). 
I also love most [Free League](https://freeleaguepublishing.com) products (especially *Blade Runner* RPG). I find their systems hit the right spot between rule complexity and ease of play. 
Beware: you will find no *D&D* here. I've played enough of that in my life, now I need something else.

What I look for in a solo adventure is creating a **good story**. Rules are here to help me achieve that. If they don't, I may as well ignore them.

I use a self hosted [Foundry VTT](https://foundryvtt.com) instance for my games. I find it a great Tabletop Simulator software, with the added bonus that it can be self-hosted.

The images I use in my logs are copyright of their owners. I do not earn anything from this blog, but if you are the owner and you do not want me to use the images, just say the word and I will remove them.
